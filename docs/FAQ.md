## General Questions

### What is pi-cast?

pi-cast is an interactive mathematics learning platform that combines video lessons with hands-on manipulation. Students can pause lectures to experiment with equations and see real-time visual feedback.

### How is pi-cast different from other learning platforms?

Unlike traditional video platforms, pi-cast offers:
- **Interactive blocks**: Manipulate equations during playback
- **Real-time visualization**: See graphs update as you change parameters
- **Event-driven recording**: Efficient storage with perfect synchronization
- **Smart bookmarks**: Navigate to key concepts instantly
- **Timestamped notes**: Take notes linked to specific moments

### What subjects does pi-cast cover?

Currently focused on mathematics:
- Algebra (Linear equations, Quadratics, Polynomials)
- Pre-Calculus (Functions, Trigonometry)
- Calculus (Derivatives, Integrals)
- More subjects coming soon!

### Is pi-cast free?

pi-cast offers a **freemium** model:
- **Free Tier**: Access to basic lessons and features
- **Premium Tier**: Advanced lessons, offline access, certificates
- **Institution Plans**: School and district licensing

### What devices are supported?

pi-cast works on:
- **Desktop**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions)
- **Tablet**: iPad, Android tablets (touch-optimized)
- **Mobile**: Limited functionality on phones (coming soon)

## Account & Authentication

### How do I create an account?

You can sign up using:
1. **Email**: Enter your email and verify with a code
2. **GitHub**: Connect your GitHub account for quick sign-up

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

pi-cast uses passwordless authentication:
1. Click "Sign In"
2. Enter your email
3. Check your email for a magic link
4. Click the link to sign in

### Can I change my email address?

Yes! Go to **Settings** → **Account** → **Change Email**

### How do I delete my account?

1. Go to **Settings** → **Account**
2. Scroll to "Delete Account"
3. Confirm deletion
4. Your data will be removed within 30 days

### Can I have multiple accounts?

Each person should have one account. For teachers managing multiple classes, use organizations instead.

## For Students

### How do I enroll in a lesson?

1. Browse or search for lessons
2. Click on a lesson
3. Click **Enroll** or **Start Learning**
4. The lesson is added to your dashboard

### How do I track my progress?

Visit **Dashboard** → **Progress** to see:
- Completion percentage for each lesson
- Bookmarks completed
- Total watch time
- Lessons completed

### Can I download lessons for offline viewing?

Offline viewing is available for Premium subscribers (coming soon).

### How do I create bookmarks?

During lesson playback:
1. Click the **Bookmark** button
2. Add a title and description
3. Click **Save**

### Can I export my notes?

Yes! Go to **Dashboard** → **Notes** → **Export** to download as PDF or text file.

### What happens if I lose my place in a lesson?

Your progress is automatically saved. Go to **Dashboard** → **History** to resume any lesson.

### Can I interact with equations during playback?

Yes! Click on any equation block to:
- Adjust variable sliders
- See real-time graph updates
- Experiment without losing your place

### How do I ask questions about a lesson?

Use the **Questions** tab on each lesson page (if enabled by the teacher).

## For Teachers

### How do I become a teacher?

1. Create a student account
2. Go to **Settings** → **Account**
3. Click "Request Teacher Status"
4. Complete the application
5. Wait for admin approval (24-48 hours)

### How do I create a lesson?

1. Click **Create** → **New Lesson**
2. Fill in lesson details
3. Add equation, chart, and description blocks
4. Arrange blocks on the canvas
5. Click **Record** to start recording

### What equipment do I need?

Minimum requirements:
- Computer with modern browser
- Microphone (built-in or external)
- Stable internet connection

Recommended:
- External USB microphone
- Quiet recording space
- Second monitor for monitoring

### Can I edit my lesson after publishing?

Yes! Go to your lesson and click **Edit**. Changes create a new version while preserving the original.

### How do I see how students are doing?

Access **Dashboard** → **Analytics** to view:
- View counts
- Completion rates
- Average ratings
- Student feedback

### Can I charge for my lessons?

Premium lesson monetization is coming soon. Teachers can set prices and earn revenue share.

### What content is allowed?

Allowed:
- Original educational content
- Public domain materials
- Properly licensed content

Not allowed:
- Copyrighted material without permission
- Inappropriate content
- Misleading information

### How do I report inappropriate content?

Click the **Report** button on any lesson or contact support@pi-cast.com

## Technical Support

### The video won't play. What should I do?

Try these steps:
1. Refresh the page
2. Check your internet connection
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try a different browser
5. Disable browser extensions

### Audio is not working. How do I fix it?

1. Check that your device is not muted
2. Verify browser has microphone permissions
3. Test microphone in browser settings
4. Try a different browser

### The interactive features aren't responding. What's wrong?

1. Refresh the page
2. Check your internet connection
3. Ensure JavaScript is enabled
4. Try a different browser

### My progress isn't saving. What should I do?

1. Wait a moment for sync
2. Check your internet connection
3. Ensure you're logged in
4. Refresh the page

### The page loads slowly. How can I improve performance?

1. Close unnecessary browser tabs
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check your internet speed
4. Try a different browser

### I'm getting an error message. What do I do?

1. Note the exact error message
2. Refresh the page
3. Try the action again
4. If it persists, contact support with:
   - Error message
   - What you were doing
   - Browser and device info

## Privacy & Security

### How is my data protected?

pi-cast uses:
- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Secure storage with encryption at rest
- Regular security audits
- Compliance with GDPR and COPPA

### What data do you collect?

We collect:
- Account information (name, email)
- Learning progress and activity
- Device and browser information
- Usage analytics

We do NOT sell your personal data.

### Can I download my data?

Yes! Go to **Settings** → **Privacy** → **Download Data** to get a copy of all your data.

### How do I protect my account?

- Use a strong, unique password (if using password auth)
- Enable two-factor authentication (coming soon)
- Don't share your login credentials
- Log out on shared devices

### Is pi-cast COPPA compliant?

Yes! pi-cast is COPPA compliant for students under 13. Parental consent is required.

### How do I report a security vulnerability?

Email security@pi-cast.com with details. We appreciate responsible disclosure.
